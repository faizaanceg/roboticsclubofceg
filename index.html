Robotics Club of CEG

About the club

Robotics club of CEG is a student organization established in 2007 by our alumni which aims to bridge the gap between academic and practical knowledge, by familiarizing students in the field and guiding them on various projects undertaken during their course of study. The club works towards nurturing the technical minds of the college by providing a dias for them to showcase their talents. The skills acquired by the students help them to be industry ready and improve their prospects of employability.

AI Robotic Hand

AI Robotic Hand is our first ever biggest attempt in the field of Image processing and Artificial Intelligence. Our members have developed a Robotic hand that can interactively playhand cricket with humans. Image processing is carried out using OpenCV libraries. The feed of the human hand is taken from a Logitech webcam. The method of convex hull is used to find the number displayed by human fingers. This is fed to the algo which efficiently calculates and directs the animatronic finger with a suitable number that is to be displayed. The algorithm is developed both for batting and bowling.

Biped

Biped is one among the significant projects carried out by the members of Robotics club of CEG. It involves designing and fabricating a two legged walking robot with 6 DOF. The robot can be controlled wirelessly from any smart device. It has six high torque servos and good quality servo brackets which forms the firm body. The high current driver circuits for servos are designed by our members using power transistors and regulators. The entire system is powered by using a computer SMPS. It has the capability to be controlled over any smart device. An android application has also been developed, which makes it easy to be controlled from our smart phones.

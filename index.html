About us

Some words about the club

Robotics club of CEG is a student organization established in 2007 by our alumni, aims to bridge the gap between academic and practical knowledge, by familiarizing students in the field and guiding them on various projects undertaken during their course of study. The Club works towards nurturing the technical minds of the college by providing a dias for them to showcase their talents. The skills acquired by the students help them to be industry ready and improve their prospects of employability.
